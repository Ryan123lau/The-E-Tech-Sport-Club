Gameroom

Video games, interactive games, a bowling lane, and shooting gallery are the main source of entertainment. Pool tables and ping pong tables are available for hourly rental.

Pools

There are three pools on the E-Tech Sport Club Property. The Central Pool has a water slide, four hot-tubs, a waterfall, and shallow shelf foe small children. The River Pool is a flowing pools with side pools for relaxation.

Gym

The work out room at the E-Tech Sport Club overlooks the ocean. Exercise using treadmills, elliptical machines, stationary bicycles, weight machines, and free weights while enjoying a view of the beautiful white sand beach. Rise and shine exercise programs begin at 8am with pool aerobics and beach yoga.

Tennis Courts

The Tennis courts are located on top of the parking garage. There are five tennis courts and onr practices half court with a ball machine. Tennis balls and tennis lessons registration is available at the Sports Club activities desk.
